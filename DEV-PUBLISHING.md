## Before publishing

1. Confirm that the raw GitHub cover URL in the article frontmatter renders in DEV. Do not insert the image again in the article body.
2. Preview on desktop and mobile, especially the cover crop and short one-line paragraphs.
3. Keep `published: false` until the preview is approved.
4. If this article first appears elsewhere, add that original page as `canonical_url` in the frontmatter. Otherwise, omit it.
5. Confirm the four tags: `ai`, `programming`, `discuss`, `softwaredevelopment`.
6. Optionally end the post with: "Where do you draw the line between an abstraction you can trust and one you no longer understand well enough to use responsibly?"

The AI-assistance disclosure is already included at the end of the article.

Suggested image description if the artwork is reused inline: "A comic workshop pageant of programming history, from a punch-card mechanic and cable conductor to a container-ship captain and an AI-era engineer inspecting the work of tiny assistants."
